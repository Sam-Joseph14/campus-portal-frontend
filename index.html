About the Portal

Campus Smart Service Portal is a centralized digital platform designed to simplify and modernize the process of reporting and managing campus-related issues. It enables students to submit service requests such as WiFi, hostel, or canteen issues through an intuitive interface. The system improves communication, ensures transparency, and enhances overall campus efficiency.
